A Minecraft Bedrock GUI-Based Addon maker, for Windows, Linux, and Chromebook.

About bedrockR

Minecraft Bedrock Addons can be confusing, tedious, and annoying. Coupled with Microsoft & Mojang's constant depercating, and changing, some addons can be bad. bedrockR is what I'm hoping can eliminate those challenges with a useful user interface, and regular updates.

bedrockR is heavily inspired by MCreator, and how its bedrock building is very neglected; being out of date, and lacking many features.

bedrockR's goal is to become what MCreator couldn't do, and be a fully featured bedrock addon maker, with many elements that most people probably dont know about.
